How To Buy

Download MetaMask

Install the MetaMask browser extension or mobile app to create a digital wallet for securely storing your cryptocurrencies and interacting with blockchain applications.

Secure Base (BASE ETH)

Purchase Basechain Ethereum (BASE), the foundational currency required for transactions on the base network where $POLA operates. BASE ETH acts as the "gas" for transactions, including buying $POLA.

Buy $POLA

Use your newly acquired BASE ETH to purchase $POLA tokens through a cryptocurrency exchange or decentralized exchange platform that lists $POLA.

Add To Your Wallet

Import $POLA tokens into your MetaMask wallet by adding the pola token's contract address. This allows you to view and manage your $POLA holdings directly within MetaMask.
